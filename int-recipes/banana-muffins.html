Banana Muffins!

Description

These banana muffins are moist and delicious. This is an easy recipe for kids to help make and perfect for using up ripe bananas. They're wonderful as-is or with walnuts or chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Leaveners
  • Salt
  • Bananas
  • Sugar
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Sift dry ingredients together in a bowl
  2. Fold dry mixture into wet mixture
  3. Spoon batter into muffin tins
  4. Bake muffins in preheated oven
